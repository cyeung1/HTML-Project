Purpose

This is a website containing information about the most popular search engines. Even though there are many types of search engines we will only focus on one type, the web search engine or Internet search engine. Web search engines have a huge influence in our lives. Without them finding information will be much more difficult and timely. Also, the developers can choose to adjust what kind of information is available. They can also block or promote information. Thus companies that can put their advertisements on the web page recieve big exposure.
